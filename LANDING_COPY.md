# FAQ

# 1. What exactly does the audit analyze?

The audit analyzes:
- customer reviews
- sentiment trends
- fake review signals
- pricing competitiveness
- repeated customer complaints
- conversion risk indicators

The goal is to identify issues affecting trust and purchase decisions.

---

# 2. Does this work for Shopify and Amazon products?

Yes.

The tool is designed for:
- Shopify stores
- Amazon listings
- DTC ecommerce brands
- ecommerce product pages with review data

---

# 3. How long does the audit take?

Most audits complete in:
```text
under 60 seconds.
```

Large review datasets may take slightly longer.

---

# 4. Is the audit generated by AI?

Yes.

The system uses AI models to:
- analyze review sentiment
- detect suspicious patterns
- summarize customer complaints
- generate recommendations

However, scoring logic and validation rules are also applied separately for consistency.

---

# 5. Do I need technical knowledge to use it?

No.

The product is designed for:
- founders
- operators
- growth teams
- ecommerce managers

No analytics or technical background is required.
