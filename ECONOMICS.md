# Unit Economics & Revenue Model

# Assumptions

Since this is an early-stage product, the following numbers are directional estimates based on:
- SaaS conversion benchmarks
- ecommerce lead generation funnels
- founder-led sales motion
- AI infrastructure pricing
- fintech/credit marketplace economics

The goal is not precision.
The goal is to test whether the business model can realistically support scale.

# Biggest Economic Risk

## The Real Risk Is NOT Traffic

The biggest risk is:

```text
audit insights are interesting
but do not create buying intent.
```

If users:
- consume audits passively
- never book consultations
- never use financing

then the funnel collapses.

---

# What Makes the Model Work

The product succeeds economically if:

```text id="8zprv7"
1. audits expose painful business problems
2. founders trust the recommendations
3. Credex becomes the “next logical step”
4. financing products solve immediate operational pain
```

---

# Final Assessment

## Why This Could Work

The model works because:
- acquisition can initially stay low-cost
- audits create high-intent leads
- LTV of ecommerce financing customers is meaningful
- AI automation scales efficiently

The most important metric is NOT traffic.
